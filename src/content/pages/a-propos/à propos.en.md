You're on the website of **BUM-BUM: the network of community bike shops in Montreal**.

A **community bike shop** is a fixed or mobile space run by an organization that offers the **community** a place for **assisted self-repair** of bikes, **free of charge or at low cost**. By '_assisted self-repair_', we mean that people work on their own bike while being helped along by the shop's mechanics — hobbyists of varying skill levels, or professionals. **Resources**, **knowledge**, and **tools** related to bikes are **shared** there.

BUM-BUM's goal is to build a lasting network that supports, represents, and raises the profile of Montreal's community bike shops. BUM-BUM aims to stay accessible, grounded in the reality of these shops, in solidarity, and resilient.
