You're on the website of **BUM-BUM: Montreal's Community Bike Shop Network**.

A **community bike shop** is a fixed or mobile space run by an organization that offers the **community** a place for **assisted self-repair** of bikes, **free of charge or at low cost**. By '_assisted self-repair_', we mean that people work on their own bike while being helped along by the shop's mechanics — amateurs, volunteers or professionals of varying skill levels. **Resources**, **knowledge**, and **tools** related to bikes are **shared** openly in these spaces.

BUM-BUM's goal is to build a lasting network that supports, represents, and elevates Montreal's community bike shops. BUM-BUM aims to remain accessible, grounded in the reality of these shops, in solidarity, and resilient.
